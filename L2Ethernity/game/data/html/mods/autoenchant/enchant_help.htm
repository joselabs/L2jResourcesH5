About the service

What kind of service? This service allows you to instantly enchant item to a given level with a certain chance.
What is difference between enchant in service? Enchant used in the service are completely identical to usual enchants and have same chance.
How does it work? When you click on the button, the service performs a cycle of enchant using the parameters you set. as a result, you will receive a report on success and costs.
What is service mechanics? Enchant cycle continues until: > Enchant level will not reach the level you set > Amiunt of enchnat items used will not reach the limit you set > Enchant conditions are met
