Commands Info

.menu : Character control panel
.online : Shows how many players are online in game
.repair : Repairing character located in same account
.password : Changing Password of your Account
.sellbuffs : Sell your buffs
.buffshield : Protects you from unwanted buffs
.combine : Combines all same type talismans, into 1 with the total duration
.dressme : Change your visual texture appearance of Armor/Weapon/Cloak!
.expon/.expoff : Experience gain On/Off
.deposit/.withdraw : Warehouse functions
.hellbound : Hellbound level display
.teletocl : Teleportation to clan leader
.aa/.makeaa : Convert the ancient adena
.7rb : Displays status of the quest for 7 bosses
.stats : Personal stats of target
.notraders : Hide/Show all players traders
.buffanimation : Disable buffs animation
