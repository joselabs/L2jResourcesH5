.engage: Tries to engage your target, must be on friendlist (wedding related)
.divorce: Tries to divorce your target, must be married (wedding related)
.gotolove: Tries to teleport to your partner, must be married
.tradeoff: Blocks all trade invites and other invites from other players
.tradeon: Enables all the invite prompts (default)
.accD: Toggles whether you want either (or none) of your hair accessories to be displayed on your character
.lockdown: Locks down your entire account for time specified in hours (like 4.2). when your account is locked down some functions are disabled such as trades, buy/sell, enchant/aug/attribute/destroy, etc, basically to prevent you from getting 'scammed' (by yourself)
.lockdowntime: Displays to you how long your account is locked down for, if it's locked down
.adenaclanwh: Used for depositing large amounts of Adena to your clan warehouse since the in-game warehouse deposit only supports 2.1b at a time
