 Here goes the server rules;
1. %class% Disrespectful attitude or any aggressive behavior towards Staff Members is forbidden. Admins or GM's can punish anyone breaking the rules without further notice.Punishment is ranging from temporary ban to permanent ban on the user's machine. These rules also apply outside of the game (forums, e-mails, discord etc).
2. Using a program that allows for automated gameplay, such as bot(L2Walker, Andrenaline) is strictly forbidden.
3. You are responsible for sharing your account. GMs are not required to assist you in recovering stolen items in case of account sharing fraud. It is absolutely essential to keep account information to yourself!
4. Active exploitation of broken or unintended game mechanics (i.e. bugs, glitches) is not tolerated.You should inform Admins/GM's immediately. We reward anyone who help us improve.
5. It is forbidden to share information about an exploit with anyone except the staff.
6. Actively engaging in slander, racism, real life threats, insults or highly disruptive behavior in in-game chat is against the rules.
7. It is forbidden to impersonate a staff member in any way; Punishment is a direct ban without any warnings.
8. All shouts in game are in English. If caught shouting at Hero or All chat in other language rather than English, the punishment is chat ban.
9. Flaming the server in any way(shouts,social media etc) or anyone from the Staff will cause in a chat ban and eventually in a perma ban.
10. Real money trading, selling characters, materials for real money will result in an account ban.
11. If you need support please send a Forum, Discord, Facebook pm or make a petition ingame.Pms in game will not be accepted and irrelevant petitions will be rejected.
12. Do not advertise other servers in any way, and do not refer to names of different servers either.
13. If you do any mistake that includes buying the wrong item, trading the wrong person or enchanting an item by mistake Gms have no responsibility
14. Scam shops are not allowed (misleading shop title, buy instead of sell and vice-versa, lying about price and so on).
