Custom user commands:
Command Function
.engage tries to engage your target, must be on friendlist (wedding related)
.divorce tries to divorce your target, must be married (wedding related)
.gotolove tries to teleport to your partner, must be married (obviously)
.tradeoff blocks all trade invites and other invites from other players Site
.tradeon enables all the invite prompts (default)
.accD toggles whether you want either (or none) of your hair accessories to be displayed on your character
.roll rolls a random number betwen 1 and 100 (If you buy a Dice and roll that, it rolls 1-6)
.lockdown (timeInHours) locks down your entire account for time specified in hours (like 4.2). when your account is locked down some functions are disabled such as trades, buy/sell, enchant/aug/attribute/destroy, etc, basically to prevent you from getting 'scammed' (by yourself)
.lockdowntime displays to you how long your account is locked down for, if it's locked down
.adenaclanwh (amountOfAdenas) used for depositing large amounts of Adena to your clan warehouse since the in-game warehouse deposit only supports 2.1b at a time
