All positions are sorted by their priority.
1. Numbered: Rewards that apply for one certain position - For example '5.' will reward the fifth best player/team (based on score) in the event; '2.' will reward the second best player/team. Players that have been rewarded by any of these Numbered positions will not get rewards from any of General (Winner, Looser,..) or Range (2. - 5.) positions.
2. Range: Rewards that apply for some range of positions - such as 2.-5. will reward 2nd, 3rd, 4th and 5th best players (based on score) in the event. Players that have been rewarded by any of Ranged or General positions will not receive any Ranged rewards.
3. General: Rewards that apply for a certain group. For example 'Looser' are all players/teams that didn't win the match (2., 3., 4.,...) and weren't been rewarded by any of Numbered or Range positions.
Important: If you have, for example, specified position 'Looser' of type General and position '4.' of type Numbered, the 4th best player in the event will receive only the reward of type Numbered, because Numbered rewards have the biggest priority. Basically, every player will always receive rewards only from one Position (Winner, Looser, 2., 8., 4-8.,...).
